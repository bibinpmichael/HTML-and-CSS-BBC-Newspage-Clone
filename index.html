Corgi Stuck on Ceiling After Inhaling Helium

Edinburgh, Scotland — In a bizarre incident, a one-year-old Corgi named Bernard has been stuck on the ceiling of his owner’s home for 12 hours after accidentally inhaling helium from balloons at a child’s birthday party. The playful pup floated up to the ceiling, leaving his owners in a tricky situation.

Local veterinarian Dr. Levi Tate informed the BBC that while rare, similar cases have occurred. He mentioned a 1954 incident where a Chihuahua named ChiChi floated from Mexico City to Phoenix, Arizona, after a similar helium mishap.
